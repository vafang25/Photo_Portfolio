Vivian Fang is a rising junior at the Trinity School on the Upper West Side of Manhattan in New York City. This summer, she is participating in the Girls Who Code Immersion Program at Morgan Stanley, where students learn programming languages such as Python, Arduino-C, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Outside of coding, she is a portrait phographer that works with both digital and film cameras. She prints photos in the darkroom and scans negatives to process them in Adobe Photoshop.
